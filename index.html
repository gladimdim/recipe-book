Book for Cook

Book for Cook helps to organize recipes for different dishes.

Welcome to Offtie official page.

Thank you for interesting in app "Book for Cook". This app is helpful for people who like to cook and have a lot of recipes. With this app you can easily store your recipes grouped by categories. Each recipe has a list of ingridients and description (steps to cook). App also allows to set reminder with a list of groceries needed for recipe. It is also possible to share whole recipe book or only one recipe via email.

FAQ

  1. Question: Does Offtie require internet connection to load pages?

    Answer: No. Once the timeline is saved you do not have to be connected to internet to load pages from tweets.

  2. Question: Does Offtie posts tweets on my behalf?

    Answer: No. Offtie does not post tweets at all.

  3. Question: What is average amount of traffic used to download timeline?

    Answer: About 2Mb for 20 tweets.

  4. Question: Does Offtie saves images/videos from pages?

    Answer: No. Offtie saves only text from the page.

  5. Question: If I use Offtie to view downloaded pages - does it still connect to Internet?

    Answer: If internet connection is available - Offtie will try to load images from the page. If Internet connection is not available - only text from the page will be loaded to user.

  6. Question: Does Offtie support multiple Twitter accounts?

    Answer: Yes. Whatever amount of accounts you have added to your device - all of them can be used by Offtie.

  7. Question: Am I safe while being in roaming network from data usage by Offtie?

    Answer: No. Whenever your device has Internet connection - Offtie will use it to load images from pages. But text is not loaded from Internet after page was saved to device
